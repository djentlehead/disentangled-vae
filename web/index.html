Style Transfer Studio

Disentangled rhythm & pitch recombination

This is a rhythm/pitch recombination tool, not a "make it sound like Chopin" composer filter. It encodes a MIDI clip into two separate latent codes - one for rhythm, one for pitch - and lets you swap either half with another clip's. Try a Bach rhythm with a Chopin melody contour, or just reconstruct a single piece to hear what the model keeps and loses.

Current limits, so results make sense:

  • Only the first 32 seconds of any uploaded MIDI are used - the rest is ignored.
  • Only notes in MIDI pitch range 40–71 (roughly E2–G5) are seen by the model.
  • Output is a single instrument, binary on/off piano roll - no velocity or pedal nuance.
  • True composer-conditioned transfer is a planned v2, built on a different model that isn't wired up here yet.
